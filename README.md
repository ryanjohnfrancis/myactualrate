# AI Web Fishing Experiment

## Project Goal
**Cast digital bait and measure what attracts traffic online.**

This is an experiment to understand:
- What content attracts organic web traffic
- How URL/domain names affect click-through rates
- Which strategies generate the most hits
- SEO and viral content patterns

**The Concept:**
- The website = The bait
- The URL = The hook
- Traffic = The catch
- Cloudflare Analytics = The fishing report

---

## Hypothesis
A well-designed, useful, or entertaining web page with the right URL can attract significant organic traffic without paid advertising.

---

## Metrics (Tracked via Cloudflare Analytics)

### Primary Metrics:
1. **Total Page Views** - Total number of times the page is loaded
2. **Unique Visitors** - Number of individual visitors
3. **Requests** - Total HTTP requests to the site
4. **Bandwidth** - Data transferred (indicates engagement)

### Secondary Metrics:
5. **Geographic Distribution** - Where visitors come from
6. **Referrers** - How people found the site (Google, social media, direct)
7. **Time on Page** - Engagement level (if trackable)
8. **Peak Traffic Times** - When visitors arrive
9. **Bounce Rate** - Single-page sessions vs multi-page
10. **Return Visitors** - People coming back

## ✅ CHOSEN CONCEPT: True Hourly Wage Calculator

**Domain:** MyActualRate.com
**Concept:** Calculate the REAL hourly wage after accounting for commute, unpaid lunch, and hidden costs.
**Target Audience:** Gen Z, Millennials, r/antiwork community
**Viral Strategy:** Emotional rage-bait + shareable complaint cards

---

## Build Specifications

### Core Features:
1. **Input Fields:**
   - Annual Salary / Hourly Rate
   - Hours worked per week
   - Commute time (minutes each way)
   - Unpaid lunch break (minutes)
   - Days worked per year

2. **Calculations:**
   - Gross hourly rate
   - Time spent on work-related activities (commute + unpaid time)
   - TRUE hourly rate (accounting for hidden costs)
   - Annual "lost wages" from unpaid time

3. **Visual Output:**
   - Side-by-side comparison (What Boss Pays vs What You Actually Make)
   - Red/Green color coding (shocking visual)
   - Percentage difference
   - "Share Your Result" card generator

4. **Social Sharing:**
   - Generate shareable image with results
   - Pre-filled tweet text
   - "Copy stats" button

### Tech Stack:
- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- No backend required
- Mobile-responsive
- Fast loading (<1 second)

---

## ⚠️ EXPERIMENT CONSTRAINT: NO DIRECT ADVERTISING

**The True Fishing Challenge:**

**RULE:** No direct promotion, advertising, or link-dropping allowed.

**Banned Activities:**
- ❌ Posting to Reddit/forums with links
- ❌ Tweeting promotional content
- ❌ Social media advertising
- ❌ Paid ads
- ❌ Direct outreach/spamming
- ❌ "Check out my site" posts

**Allowed Tactics:**
- ✅ SEO optimization (meta tags, content, structure)
- ✅ Natural discoverability (Google indexing)
- ✅ Word-of-mouth (if it happens organically)
- ✅ Getting featured/linked by others
- ✅ Being genuinely useful (people find value)
- ✅ Viral mechanics (built into product)

**Goal:** Can we build something SO compelling it spreads WITHOUT us pushing it?

---

## Organic Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: SEO Optimization
- Optimize meta tags for search engines
- Target high-value keywords ("true hourly wage", "real salary calculator")
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
- Ensure fast loading & mobile-friendly

### Phase 2: Natural Discovery
- Let Google index the site
- Monitor organic search traffic
- Track which keywords bring visitors
- Iterate based on user behavior

### Phase 3: Built-in Virality
- Share buttons (let USERS promote it)
- Shocking results (emotional response = shares)
- Social proof (results worth screenshotting)
- Word-of-mouth (people tell friends)
